A step-by-step process for Excel

Using Excel we check the data for nulls, misspelled words, mistyped numbers, extra spaces and characters, duplicates, inconsistent strings and date formats, labels.

Processes for Excel

  1. Downloaded and extract the data from the last 12 months of historical trips, organized them locally and separated XLS and CSV files folders.
  2. Cleaned the data for the month of 07/2022, column by column using pivot tables in Excel Repeated for the next 11 months up to the most recent record.
  3. Create new column “ride_length” to know the time each rider spent with their bike.
  4. Create new column “day_of_week” to know the day each rider used the bike.
  5. Calculated max, and the mean of of “ride_length”.
  6. Calculated the mode of “day_of_week”.
  7. Created a pivot table to visualize the average weekly “ride_length” between casual riders and member riders.
